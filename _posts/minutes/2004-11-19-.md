X.org compositing
-----------------

Command to turn everything on

`xcompmgr -cCfF -r7 -o.65 -l-10 -t-8 -D7`

Xorg.conf

`Section "Extentions"`  
`    Option "Composite" "Enable"`  
`    Option "RENDER" "Enable"`  
`EndSection`

For NVIDIA cards, add this option in your "Device Section":

`Section "Device"`  
`    Option "RenderAccel" "true"`  
`EndSection`
